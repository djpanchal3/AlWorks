About ALWorks

Built on Metal,
Proven by Projects

ALWorks is Ahmedabad's premier aluminium section fabricator and supplier, serving over 500 projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors since 2006.

From precision-extruded profiles to full turnkey installation, we partner with architects, contractors, and interior designers to bring high-performance aluminium solutions that meet international standards and withstand India's diverse climate demands.
